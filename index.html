The Art Of Eight Limbs

Here at Muay Thai Cork we specialise in the art of combat. Muay Thai, also known as Thai boxing, is a traditional martial art from Thailand that emphasizes striking techniques using fists, elbows, knees, and shins. It is known as the "Art of Eight Limbs" due to the use of these eight points of contact. Muay Thai is not only a sport but also a cultural practice that incorporates rituals, music, and ceremonies. We welcome all people from all types of backgrounds, whether you're new or returning to combat sports. For more information don't hesitate to contact us through phone or email or pop in to the gym for a chat with one of our trainers!

Our Core Values

At our gym, Muay Thai is more than just a workout. It's a way to build confidence, discipline and real strenght, Both mentally and physically. We focus on creating a welcoming, no-ego environment where everyone, from complete veginners to experienced fighters can train hard, support each other and grow. Our approach blends authentic Muay Thai technique with modern fitness principles, helping you push your limits, develop resilience and become the best version of yourself, on and off the mats.

Classes

Adults Muay Thai (Fitness)

Adults Muay Thai Classes for begginers or returning members who are looking for more of a fitness class. In this class you will be learning punching, kicking, clenching and basic overall fitness. A very intensive class so make sure to bring water.

€10 Euro per person (1st Class Free for Newcomers)

Adults Muay Thai (Sparring)

Adults Muay Thai classes, for people of all experiences. Anyone welcome to our sparring classes. We provide head gear and shin pads. Bring a mouth guard or you will not be allowed to spar. All sparring is light. A 30 minute warm up is included in this class which would entail 60/60/60 (push ups, sit ups, burpees) and 50 kicks per leg on a bag.

€10 Euro per person

Boxing

Boxing class, which includes a 30 minute run, 60/60/60 for warm ups and lessons from one of our specialised trainers. Sparring also included at the end of the class for anyone who is interested but it is not mandatory. Headguards provided. Mouthguard required.

€10 Euro per person

Kids Classes

Our Muay Thai classes for kids offer a fun, safe and structured environment where children can learn the fundamentals of Muay Thai. Helps to develop strength, coordination, discipline and self confidence. Whether your child is full of energy or looking to gain confidence, our classes will provide a supportive space for growth and personal development.

€5 Euro per child
