Muay Thai Cork

The Art Of Eight Limbs

Here at Muay Thai Cork we specialise in the art of combat. Muay Thai, also known as Thai boxing, is a traditional martial art from Thailand that emphasizes striking techniques using fists, elbows, knees, and shins. It is known as the "Art of Eight Limbs" due to the use of these eight points of contact. Muay Thai is not only a sport but also a cultural practice that incorporates rituals, music, and ceremonies. We welcome all people from all types of backgrounds, whether you're new or returning to combat sports. For more information don't hesitate to contact us through phone or email or pop in to the gym for a chat with one of our trainers!
