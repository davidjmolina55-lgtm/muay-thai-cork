About Muay Thai

Muay Thai, also known as Thai boxing, is a traditional martial art from Thailand that emphasizes striking techniques using fists, elbows, knees, and shins. It is known as the "Art of Eight Limbs" due to the use of these eight points of contact. Muay Thai is not only a sport but also a cultural practice that incorporates rituals, music, and ceremonies.
